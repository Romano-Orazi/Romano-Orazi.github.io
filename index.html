Who I Am

I’m Romano Orazi, a freelance developer based in Rome with 7 years of experience. I started with Java (object-oriented) and later discovered the power of Google Apps Script to automate boring, bureaucratic processes. I love solving real logistic problems — from driver shift management to attendance tracking.

I work as a Java instructor (Formatemp courses) and as a freelance developer. I don't stop until the result is as close to perfection as possible. When I'm not coding, I drive sidecar tours around Rome, fix motorcycles, or ride them.

My current mission: filling the gaps in modern full‑stack development (React, Node.js, Linux) while staying true to my automation roots.
